A Tradition for over 40 years //

LePera Steel and Craftsmanship

A Perfect Fit

Keeping with our 40+ year tradition, we are one of the only seat companies still using steel exclusively for all of our seat bases. Our steel bases have helped build our reputation and year after year, have set us apart from the rest. In the last four years, there have been many changes made to Softails, Sportsters and Dynas. We've made all of the necessary modifications for the proper fit, in fact, in all three models we've made completely new steel-forming dies.

Our perfected forming process has created our company's cleanest and tightest fitting base plates ever. For us, there is simply nothing like the secure feeling you get from steel. All of our brackets are securely welded or bolted with weld nuts to each plate - something not possible with fiberglass or plastic. After checking out our seats, you'll never look at another base plate the same way again.

We only use the highest quality materials in each and every one of our seats. Looking through our catalog you will see the pride and dedication we take in combining all of the features below, into the finest custom seats money can buy.

> Comfort in each seat is produced by our specially poured high density "Marathon" molded foam foundation.

> All seats are built on a beautifully powder-coated steel base plate edged with a metal reinforced vinyl protectant.

> The bottom of every base plate is carpeted providing a friction- free surface between seat and fender.

> Every inch of material is pulled tight around the edges and trimmed leaving only the cleanest finish possible. (We know you can't see the bottom of our seats when they're on the bike, but its the little details that make a LePera seat... A LePera seat.)

> All LePera seats are covered with a premium naugahyde or top grain leather.

> All covers are double stitched with bonded polyester thread.

Our craftsmanship, as well as cutting edge design, have put us at the forefront of the custom Harley seat world. Focusing on Harleys exclusively has enabled us to create the perfect relationship between seat and motorcycle. We have crafted a defining balance between form and function. Every seat we make has its own unique style that transforms the personality of your bike. Our most popular seats are based on what we call "pure form." These seats rely solely on a shape so pure, they singlehandedly create the look and flow of the motorcycle.The Silhouette, King Cobra and Bare Bones are three such designs. Their simplistic lines are what make them the most stylish seats available today. Even our touring based seats are an exercise in style, form and function. Both the Maverick and the new Sorrento are intentionally designed to compliment, not overpower the lines of the bike. Their relative low profile and non-confining contours contrast the traditional, bulky "road couches" available elsewhere. Here at LePera, we're always taking seat design to the next level. That's what has built our reputation as a trendsetter ... constantly pushing the limits of what a seat can be.
