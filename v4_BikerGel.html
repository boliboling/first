Biker Gel by LePera

Overview

Adding a new dimension to motorcycle seat comfort, Biker Gel has taken the seat world by storm. Biker Gel has a unique way of conforming to the rider, distributing weight evenly across the seat. With an amazing memory characteristic, Biker Gel never loses its shape. Biker Gel will not freeze, crack, melt or break down. And it will never leak because it is a solid gel, not a liquid gel - like so many think.

Please Note:

Don't expect a soft, pillowy feel. Biker Gel provides a firm, supportive foundation that realizes its full comfort the longer you ride.
